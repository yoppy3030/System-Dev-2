Japanese Work Culture

Understanding traditions and practices in the Japanese workplace.

Group Harmony (Wa)

Japanese workplaces emphasize teamwork and harmony. Decisions are often made collectively to maintain unity.

Senpai-Kohai Relationship

This senior-junior dynamic governs behavior in companies. Juniors respect seniors, while seniors mentor juniors.

Nomikai (Drinking Culture)

Company drinking parties are common and build camaraderie, though participation is usually voluntary.
