About JAPAN Life Manual

Helping foreigners adapt to life in Japan with ease.

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate students and professionals committed to bridging cultural gaps and offering practical guidance for people living in or moving to Japan.

Our Vision

To create a user-friendly, informative, and community-driven platform that eases the transition into Japanese society for students, workers, and travelers alike.

What We Offer

Our site provides tips on daily life, cultural norms, work and study environments, and regional customs across Japan, along with a supportive community feature.
